## 1. Get Your Free AI Key (One-Time Setup)
To use the **ChatPDF** and **Summarizer** tools, you need a free "Google Gemini API Key". This connects your app to Google's AI.

1.  **Click the Link**: When you open ChatPDF or Summarizer, you will see a link: "Get Free API Key".
    *   Or go here: [https://aistudio.google.com/app/apikey](https://aistudio.google.com/app/apikey)
2.  **Sign In**: Use your Google/Gmail account.
3.  **Create Key**: Click **"Create API Key"** -> **"Create API key in new project"**.
4.  **Copy**: Copy the string that starts with `AIza...`.
5.  **Paste**: Go back to your app and paste it into the box.

*Note: Your key is saved securely in your browser. It is never sent to us.*
